Pork Stew

Asian Malaysian Entree


Makes servings

Ingredients

  • 500 g pork belly or butt
  • 1000 ml water
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 15 ml white peppercorn
  • 5 hard boiled eggs
  • 200 g bean curd
  • 60 ml soy sauce
  • 45 ml sweet soy sauce
  • 20 ml dark soy sauce
  • salt to taste

Equipment

  • Pot

Directions

  1. Mince the garlic
  2. Cut up the pork into 2 cm cubes
  3. Boil the water


  1. Add the garlic, pork, bean curd, and hard boiled eggs. Boil for 30 minutes.
  2. Add the soy sauce, pepper, and any needed salt. Boil for 20 additional minutes.
